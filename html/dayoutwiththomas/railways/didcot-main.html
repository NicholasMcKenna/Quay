All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join us at Didcot Railway Centre for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy, including:

• Watch re-enactments of classic Thomas stories with The Fat Controller
• Unlimited train rides behind Thomas
• Entertainment from Rusty and Dusty
• Balloon modellers
• Rides behind other steam engines
• Children’s activities
• Explore the Engine Shed
• Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities

Gates open at 10.00am and the fun continues until 5.00pm.

Accessibility at the Station:
Access is through the subway at Didcot Parkway Station and includes a flight of steps to the Railway Centre entrance. Visitors are advised to check Didcot Railway Centre’s website for information about parking and the Access Statement.

Opening times

Parking

There is plenty of car parking in the multi-storey car park for Didcot Parkway Station – follow signs to station car park or brown signs to Didcot Railway Centre.
