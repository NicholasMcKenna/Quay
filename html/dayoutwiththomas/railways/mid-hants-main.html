All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join the Mid Hants Railway for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!
Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy including:

• Unlimited 5 minute ride behind Thomas
• Unlimited rides behind Diesel and Toad
• Join Rusty and Dusty’s storytelling and magic shows
• Vintage fairground rides
• Miniature railway
• View other engines from our viewing gallery
• Other Thomas themed activities
