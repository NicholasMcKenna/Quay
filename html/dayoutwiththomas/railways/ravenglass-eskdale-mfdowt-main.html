All aboard for My First Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join the Ravenglass & Eskdale Railway for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy, including:

• Watch re-enactments of classic stories with Thomas and The Fat Controller.
• Join Rusty and Dusty’s storytelling.
• Find out how our railway inspired the original books featuring Thomas The Tank Engine.
• Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities.

FAQs

Length of Thomas ride?
3 to 4 minutes.
Additional train rides to Dalegarth Station will also be available (40 minutes each way).

Are the Thomas rides unlimited?
Yes.

Opening and Closing time of each event:
The event will open at 09:30 and close at 17:30
The final outbound service from Ravenglass will be at 15:30 and the final inbound service from Dalegarth will be at 17:10 which will arrive at Ravenglass after the event has closed at 17:50.

Specific characters appearing at the event:
Thomas.
