All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join the South Devon Railway for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy including:

• Entry to the Buckfast Butterfly Farm and Dartmoor Otter Sanctuary
• Entry to the Totnes Rare Breeds Farm
• Re-enactments of classic Thomas stories with Thomas and The Fat Controller
• Vintage Bus Rides
• Vintage fairground rides
• Miniature railway
• Face painting
• Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities

FAQs

Length of Thomas ride?
15 minutes on the Thomas Shuttle and 1 hour and 15 minutes on the ‘Big Train’ to Totnes and back.

Are the Thomas rides unlimited?
Yes.

Specific characters appearing at the event:
Thomas and Toad.
