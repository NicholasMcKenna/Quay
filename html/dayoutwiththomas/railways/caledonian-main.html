All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join the Caledonian Railway for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy at Brechin Station, including:

• Train journey behind Thomas the Tank Engine
• Meet Percy
• Meet Diesel
• Face Painting
• Join Rusty and Dusty’s storytelling
• Fairground rides & Bouncy Castles
• Miniature Railway & Brakevan Rides
• Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities

Classic bus rides around the local area will also be departing from Bridge of Dun Station (available only to open return ticket holders).

FAQs

Length of Thomas ride?
50 minute return, 20 minutes between stations.

Are the Thomas rides unlimited?
No, one per ticket.

Opening and Closing time of each event
9:30am to 4:30pm

Specific characters appearing at the event:
Thomas the Tank Engine, Percy, Diesel.

Disabled Passengers:
To book tickets in the disabled access saloon, please contact Quaytickets on 0843 208 1840 and we will be happy to help you over the phone. Please note that tickets sold for the disabled saloon are available only as direct returns.
