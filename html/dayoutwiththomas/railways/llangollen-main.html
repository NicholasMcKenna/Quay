All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join Llangollen Railway for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy, including:

• Unlimited rides with Thomas
• Meet Diesel and Daisy
• Watch re-enactments of classic Thomas stories with Thomas and The Fat Controller
• Join Rusty and Dusty's storytelling
• Ride behind the big steam train
• Colouring and toys at Carrog
• Stilt walker and jugglers
• Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities
