All aboard for a Day Out With Thomas!

Activities

Join The East Anglian Railway Museum for trainloads of fun and a day of anniversary celebrations!

Take a train ride with Thomas, the number 1 blue engine. Meet The Fat Controller who will be keeping all of the celebrations in order and join Sodor Station Sweepers Rusty & Dusty in a birthday singalong.

Plus lots more to enjoy, including:

•Unlimited rides behind Thomas, Percy & Daisy
•Meet Toby & Henrietta
•Watch re-enactments of classic Thomas stories with Thomas, Rusty & Dusty and The Fat Controller
•Join Rusty and Dusty for The Fat Controller’s storytelling
•Model Railway Display
•Watch the Rusty & Dusty Show
•Other Thomas & Friends-themed activities
•Balloon Modellers
