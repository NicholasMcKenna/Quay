Welcome to your Intranet!

The only place where you can book tickets to see a wide range of shows at The Lowry at an exclusive rate. Simply enter your company’s code and book away!

When you enter your code, you will be taken to the discounts page, this will display all our shows with an exclusive rate available, you can book as many tickets as you want, but please note some shows may have set dates that the offer is available. If you would like to book a show that doesn’t have a discount available please head to thelowry.com.

If you have any questions or for further information please contact groups@thelowry.com.

Don’t forget you can pre-order your interval drinks and ice-creams at the bar before the show at our Pier Eight Bar and Restaurant, where you can also enjoy pre-theatre dining and afternoon tea.
